## The Vision

Wildlife monitoring and ecosystem management is labor-intensive and expensive. Autonomous systems have the potential to:

- **Scale observation** — Monitor larger areas and more individuals than human observers alone
- **Reduce disturbance** — Collect data without disrupting natural behaviors  
- **Enable persistence** — Operate continuously in conditions humans cannot sustain
- **Democratize access** — Make sophisticated monitoring available to conservation efforts worldwide
